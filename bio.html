A little about Me...

Born and raised in Philadelphia I will always love Philly sports and have a special place in my heart for the "City of Brotherly Love." Since leaving Philly for college I have embraced my passion for travel and have lived in DC, Miami, San Diego, New York City and San Francisco. Like my living situations, my professional experience has been diverse as well. I started my career in corporate finance, worked in sales and marketing before finding my passion as an entrepreneur & product manager. I have been very fortunate to have founded 2 start-ups over the past 8 years with some absolutely amazing people. In reflecting on my experiences, I have realized that the journey is the reward. I am excited to embark on the new challenge of learning to code with the hope that it will enable me to prototype my next entrepreneurial venture. Cheers!
